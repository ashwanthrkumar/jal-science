Jal Science's innovative sewage treatment Solution, inspired by a cow's digestive system, Our Bio-Pure Septic Tank involved closely examining the mechanisms involved in anaerobic digestion in the stomach of a cow. As everyone knows, grass is converted into milk, meat, and wool in a cow's ruminant stomach. We utilized the exact same ruminant stomach process to biologize the same process to turn “waste water” into “Treated water”. Organic fecal content is broken down into treated water using our natural digester model, which is based on the biology of a cow's stomach. What happens is that the sewage you put in gets transformed to water, a small amount of gas, and very little sludge (digester) when we add anaerobic bacteria catalyst seed (one time addition) from a cow dung substrate.

Sewage Treatment Technology
Jal Science: India's First Modular Concrete Net zero Sewage Treatment Solution

Revolutionize wastewater management with Jal Sciences' advanced sewage treatment plant! Inspired by the ingenious digestive system of a cow, our technology utilizes anaerobic bio digestion reactor technology (ABR) to achieve unparalleled efficiency and sustainability.

  • An RCC (Reinforced Cement Concrete) bio-Pure septic tank treats sewage and wastewater through a combination of physical and biological processes.
  • Wastewater enters the tank through an inlet chamber, where heavy solids settle.
  • Inside the tank, anaerobic bacteria break down organic matter without oxygen, reducing sludge volume and converting waste into gases like methane and carbon dioxide.
  • Solids settle at the bottom as sludge, and lighter materials form scum, while the treated liquid, or effluent, moves to the next chamber.
  • Some systems have a secondary chamber for further treatment.
  • The effluent exits through an outlet chamber with a filter, trapping any remaining particles.
  • Finally, the treated effluent is discharged into the soil through a soak pit or leach field, or reused for irrigation, depending on the system design and local regulations.
  • This process significantly reduces pathogens and pollutants, making the water safe for disposal or reuse.
  • Treated water can be used for gardening or groundwater recharge.
  • Advanced Filtration Technology: Ensures clean and safe water storage.
  • Robust Design: Built to last with high-quality, eco-friendly materials.
  • Customizable Capacities: Standard options and bespoke designs to suit your specific requirements.
