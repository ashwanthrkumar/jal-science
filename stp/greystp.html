What is Greywater?

Greywater is wastewater generated from household activities such as bathing, washing dishes, and laundry. Unlike blackwater (from toilets), greywater can be treated and reused for non-potable purposes, significantly cutting down on freshwater usage.

Benefits of Greywater Recycling

  • Water Conservation: Reduces the demand for freshwater by recycling up to 70% of household water.
  • Cost Savings: Lowers water and sewage bills through reduced freshwater consumption.
  • Environmental Impact: Decreases the burden on local water resources and wastewater treatment facilities.
  • Sustainability: Supports green building certifications and enhances corporate social responsibility.
