India's First Non-electric Modular Concrete Sewage Treatment Solution

An innovative advanced sewage treatment solution that uses Anaerobic Biodigestion Reactor Technology (ABRT) that converts household sewage water into clear, odourless water. Treated water can be used for gardening or groundwater recharge. Bioman, non-filling bio-tank provides you with an economic and environmentally friendly solution for wastewater treatment. It's an Easy, convenient and painless way of sewage treatment./p>
